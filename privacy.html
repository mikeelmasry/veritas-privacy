Privacy Policy for Veritas

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

Thank you for using Veritas, a Coptic Orthodox Bible study app developed by Michael Elmasry. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains in plain terms how Veritas handles your information.

The short version

Veritas does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. Your questions and study sessions stay between you and the app.

What Veritas does not collect

Veritas does not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location
  • Device identifiers used for tracking or advertising
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports tied to your identity

There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no user profiles. The app is free to use and contains no advertising.

How your questions are processed

When you ask a question in Veritas, your question is sent directly from your device to Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic processes the request and returns an answer, which Veritas displays to you within the app.

I do not operate a backend server that receives, logs, or stores your questions. The communication happens directly between your device and Anthropic.

Anthropic has its own privacy practices governing API requests. You can review them here:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Commercial Terms of Service

Under Anthropic's standard API terms, content sent through the Claude API is not used to train Anthropic's models.

Patristic source content

Veritas references writings from the Church Fathers and Coptic Orthodox patristic tradition. This content is part of the app itself and does not involve any data collection from you.

Children's privacy

Veritas is suitable for users of all ages. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated in the future, the revised version will be posted at this same URL with a new effective date. Material changes will be communicated through the app or its App Store listing.
